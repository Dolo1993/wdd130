Overview

Purpose

This site is designed to help people deepen their understanding of Christianity and aid in learning the Scriptures in the most effective and efficient way through the help of the Holy Spirit. In addition, this site is here to help Churches, organizations, and individuals who are interested in learning more about our Heavenly Father and His Son Jesus Christ. Just imagine the last time you were searching for a particular book and verse within the scriptures, and the length of time it takes you to find that book and verse(s). With my company, just a click would take you to your desired book and verse(s). I develop effective and efficient scriptural apps that are easy to use for churches, homes, schools, and personal reading.

Audience

This website is available to people from all walks of life regardless of sex, culture, or religion who are in search of not only learning about our Heavenly Father and His Son Jesus Christ but also getting close to him to strengthen their spiritual well-being through the power of the Holy Spirit through scriptural studies and asking GOD for his divine will to reveal that absolute truth to you. “Verily I say, men should be anxiously engaged in a good cause, and do many things of their own free will, and bring to pass much righteousness” (Doctrine & Covenants 58:27).

Home page

Koyo Village of Religion, is a place of effectively and efficiently learning about Heavenly Father and His Son Jesus Christ through the Holy Scriptures.
According to the Book of James 1:5 “If any of you lack wisdom, let him ask of God, that giveth to all men liberally and upbraideth not; and it shall be given him”. Hence this site is here to help you discover and live the absolute truths that will guide you in this mortality and subsequently lead you to the presence of God after this life. Sometimes, you might ask yourself a question like “how can I know?” you can know this message (God’s word) is true. If you ask our Heavenly Father in prayer, you can receive an answer from Him through the Holy Ghost. The Holy Ghost is also called the Spirit of God, and one of His roles is to witness, or testify, of the truth. As taught in the Bible, “The fruit of the Spirit is love, joy, peace, longsuffering, gentleness, goodness, faith, meekness, goodness, and temperance” (Galatians 5:22-23).

Distinguishing between our Heavenly Father and Son His Jesus Christ.
Oftentimes, most people get confused with the difference between our Heavenly Father (God) and His Son Jesus Christ who is also God. Some people believed that there has been no point in time that anyone has seen God while other believed that Moses saw God face to face. Therefore this site is here to provide you with sufficient scriptural knowledge on how to distinguish between these two individuals and aid you with simple and clear answers surrounding their attributes. Before beginning any search on this page I encourage you to pray and ask God for his Divine knowledge and guidance that will help you understand and analyze every single piece of information you come across on this site.
